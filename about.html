ABOUT US

Welcome to ShopAshiorkor, a minimalist brand carefully crafted and designed for the stylish woman. Our limited ready-to-wear line is handmade in Ghana, reflecting the rich culture and artistry of Africa.

Our mission is to empower women through fashion, offering unique and high-quality clothing that celebrates individuality and elegance.

Awurabena Akoto

Founder & Creative Director

Hi! I’m Awurabena. I’m the founder, creative director and lead designer at Ashiorkor, and I love clothes — beautiful clothes. I started Ashiorkor in June 2023 to make myself and my friends functional, stylish and multi-wear wardrobe staples, a venture which is now a growing community of women passionate about expressing their unique style through timeless and thoughtfully crafted clothing. I believe style should be effortless and non-restrictive, which is why structure, pure form, refined minimalism and timeless appeal are the pillars of our process at Ashiorkor. At the heart of it, I’m an art girl drawn to nature and breathtaking landscapes, both heavy influences on the Ashiorkor design process. When I'm not in the studio, you can find me at the beach or a cosy cafe — never without coffee or a good book. Ashiorkor started with friendship as its core and we make our pieces with community, comfort and love at heart. Thank you for choosing Ashiorkor. Take what you need, and express your true self fearlessly.
Love,
Awurabena.

Our Brand Ethos

Ashiorkor, is a ready-to-wear line founded by digital marketing expert and lifestyle influencer Awurabena Akoto. Our brand ethos is simple: we make pieces that make you feel free, comfortable and chic without a need for conformity, ensuring that you can wear them all year round. To that end, Ashiorkor creates versatile, everyday all-size friendly pieces that blend vibrancy and effortless elegance for the bold and stylish woman. Each piece is thoughtfully designed and carefully handmade by our small team at our Accra studio.
